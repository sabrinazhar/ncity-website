About NCT DREAM

NCT DREAM consists of 7 members who debuted on 25th of August 2016 making them the 3rd unit to debut in NCT. The meaning behind "DREAM" is that the unit is a group where teenagers bring hopes/dreams through their songs. Originally, this sub-unit had a graduation system where the members had to graduate from the team and will be replaced with new members each year.

The maximum age to be in this sub-unit is 20 years old (Korean age) hence if any member of NCT is around that age or below, they will be able to debut in the group. But as of 2020, SM Entertainment decided to make this unit a fixed one consisting of the original members due to the fans' request. Compared to NCT 127, NCT DREAM is more famous in Korea and they are one of the few groups in the industry who were able to achieve success in such a young age.
