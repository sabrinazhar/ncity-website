About WAYV

WAYV is the latest sub-unit to debut in NCT on 17/1/2019 with 7 members. Initially, there were many speculations on whether WAYV is under NCT due to lack of interaction between the unit's members and other NCT members plus WAYV is also managed by a different label; Label V which resulted in many disagreement within the fandom. But based on NCT 2020, it is now confirmed that WAYV is a sub-unit under NCT.

WAYV is pronounced as "wavy" in English or "WeiShenV" in Korean. This sub-unit mainly promotes in China where they debuted. Their main goal is to become a global group that is well known not only in China but the rest of the world. They have become one of the famous groups in the Chinese Entertainment as of now. Recently, they made a comeback on 28/12/2022 with their fourth mini album "Phantom".
