About NCT 127

NCT 127 is the 2nd unit to debut in NCT on 7/7/2016 with the song "Fire Truck" consisting of 7 members; Taeyong, Taeil, Mark, Jaehyun, Yuta, Winwin & Haechan. On 6/1/2017, two new members; Doyoung & Johnny were added into the group during their second EP "Limitless". NCT 127 released their first full-length album "Regular-Irregular" on 12/10/2018 which was also the date where the final member of the group; Jungwoo joined.

The meaning behind the number 127 in the group's name is it represents the longitude of Seoul. The group's main concept is to spread NCT's name starting from Seoul to the rest of the world. This has been proven by the various awards & achievements they have won through out the years. They are one of the reasons why NCT has become popular around the world.
