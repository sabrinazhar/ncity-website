About NCT U


NCT U is the first sub-unit to debut in NCT with double digital singles; "The 7th Sense" on 9th of April 2016 which consists of 5 members; Taeyong, Doyoung, Ten, Jaehyun & Mark and also "WITHOUT YOU" on 10 th of April 2016 with two versions; Korean and Chinese performed by Taeil, Jaehyun, Doyoung & Kun.

The letter 'U' stands for united where all the members of NCT will be "united" in this sub-unit based on the concept provided for the song or comeback. In simpler words, this unit is a rotational unit that will vary from time to time. This implicates that there will be no exact number of members in this sub-unit and as of now, all the members of NCT have debuted in it.
