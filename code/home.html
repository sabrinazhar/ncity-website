What is NCT?

Neo Culture Technology, well known as NCT is the name of a K-Pop boy group under SM Entertainment that debuted on 9/4/2016 which consists of 23 members. There are 4 sub-units in NCT which are:

  • NCT U
  • NCT 127
  • NCT DREAM
  • WAYV

The best word to describe NCT's concept is 'limitless' because there will be limitless amount of members in the group. This means that there will be new members constantly debuting in NCT each year. NCT is the only group in the industry to have this type of concept which has made people attracted in getting to know them.
